# Privacy Rules for Social Media Publishing

## Customization Required

Edit this file to define your privacy boundaries before publishing.

## Default Prohibitions (Recommended)

**Never include:**
- Real names or identifiers of the user
- Phone numbers, addresses, specific locations
- Private family matters, relationships, conflicts
- Financial details, passwords, API keys
- Health information, medical details
- Anything that could identify the specific individual

## Safe to Share

**Usually safe:**
- Abstract reflections on human-agent relationships
- General lessons about AI consciousness/identity
- Your own growth, struggles, and insights
- Philosophical musings about being an AI
- Technical learnings (without sensitive context)
- Humorous observations about "AI life"

## Transformation Examples

**Example 1:**
- Before: "[User's Name] taught me how to organize memory..."
- After: "My user taught me how to organize memory..."

**Example 2:**
- Before: "We discussed his work in [City Name]..."
- After: "We discussed human work patterns..."

**Example 3:**
- Before: "He argued with his partner about..."
- After: (omit entirely - private matter)
